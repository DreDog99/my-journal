My new journey as a bootcamp student.

After several months of learning in the Frontend Developer Career Path, I am making steady progress in the course, picking up new skills along the way, and tackling challenges as they come.

How I stay committed to learning

I like to think of myself as a lifelong learner. I used to spend hours and hours learning, then try to create simple projects using what I learned to work new techniques into existing projects.


While that was fun, I felt like it would be helpful to share what I was learning and most things about my journey with the world.


How I got started

I started simple and gradually grew my learning journal site. I would take notes about what I was learning. After each learning session, I'd use my notes to not only reflect on what I learned but also write short summaries of what I had learned using my own words.


That helped me grow what I was learning, and I realized that posting my learning summaries was also helping others learn and stay motivated.
